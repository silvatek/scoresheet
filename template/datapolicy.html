Ice Hockey Scoresheet - Data & Privacy Policy

The scoresheet web application stores the information that you enter into it, and by entering that data you implicitly consent to us doing so. In addition, we derive additional information from some of the information you provide, and present that additional information back to you.
We hold your data purely so that you can use it. We do not use it for any other purpose.
All the data you provide is linked to a unique code, and can only be accessed by providing that code. Note, however, that there are no other controls in place: anyone with the code can access the data. If you share the codes for accessing your information with others then we can apply no restrictions on what they can do with it.
The data is stored in the europe-west2 (London) Google Cloud Platform data center.
Our systems administrators have direct access to the database, and can access all information regardless of whether the game or list codes have been explicitly shared with them. They are also able to modify the data should this be necessary, even if it is locked on the website.
